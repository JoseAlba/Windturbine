Hello There

My name is Jose and I have joined Dr.Ting in his research in the Wind Turbine Project

During my first week I was introduced to the Wind Turbine Project

My first goal was to understand the hardware/software of the hotwire calibration

I made a website to understand the content and for future use

My first attempt at making the website was dynamic approach by utilizing ASP.NET a microsoft framework that uses an MVC architectural pattern.
I ultimately decided not to use ASP because it was over-complicating a simple task which caused me to lose over 10 hours worth of work.

My second and current approach is to make a website using the traditional approach: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. I am using external libraries in order to make a responsive website.
